jpeglib
 
Cross-platform library for reading/writing jpeg images

Headers to include: jpeglib.bi
Header version: 6.2, 7.0, 8.4, 9.0 (#define __JPEGLIB_VER__ to one of 6,7,8,9 if needed; default = 9)
