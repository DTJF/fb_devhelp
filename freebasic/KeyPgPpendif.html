#endif
 
Preprocessor conditional directive

Syntax

#endif

Description

Ends a group of conditional directives

See #if, #ifdef, or #ifndef for examples of usage.

Example

#define DEBUG_LEVEL 1
#if (DEBUG_LEVEL = 1)
  'Conditional statements
#endif
