Until
 
Conditional clause used in Do..Loop statements.

Syntax

Do Until condition
or
Loop Until condition

Description

Until is used with the Do...Loop structure.

Example

Dim a As Integer

a = 1
Do
    Print "hello"
a = a + 1
Loop Until a > 10

'This will continue to print "hello" on the screen until the condition (a > 10) is met.
